老蒋每日一句｜10月

发表于 2018-10-31 | 分类于 学海无涯 ， MBA备考 ， 英语 | 阅读次数：
本文字数： 6.9k | 阅读时长 ≈ 12 分钟

这个月没坚持每日看，每日更新，月末统一整理的。
下个月恢复每日跟进。

1号

The paper shows that changing time richness, rather than income richness, is what drives changing spending patterns in the elderly.

译：这篇文章表明，促使老年人这一群体的消费模式发生变化的是时间富足程度而非金钱财富。

2号

You can type a search word into the database and find out how frequently different words were used at different epochs.

译：用户在这个数据库中输入任意搜索词，就可以了解到这些单词在不同时期的使用频率。

3号

Words associated with moral excellence, like “honesty”, “patience” and “compassion” were used much less frequently.

译：诸如“诚实”“忍耐”和“同情”这些和道德品质相关的词汇使用率就更低了。

4号 ⭐️

As it has become more individualistic, it has also become less morally aware, because social and moral fabrics are inextricably linked.

译：社会与道德密切相关，随着它逐渐变得个性主义化，人们的道德意识也日渐淡薄。

5号

Massive movements of people are likely to occur over the rest of the century because global temperatures are likely to rise by up to 5℃ due to the fact that carbon dioxide levels have risen unabated for 50 years, said Stern, who is head of the Grantham Research Institute on Climate Change.

译：人口大规模迁移可能会发生在本世纪剩下的几十年，这是因为近50年来大气二氧化碳浓度持续增长，导致全球气温可能上升高达5℃，身为格兰瑟姆气候变化研究所负责人的 Stern 表示。

6号

Agriculture could fail on a continent-wide basis and hundreds of millions of people would be rendered homeless, triggering widespread conflict.

译：整个大陆范围内的农业可能会减产，致使上亿人无家可归，从而引发大范围的冲突。

7号 周日休息

8号

Rising temperatures will shrink polar ice caps — the Arctic’s is now at its lowest since records began — and so reduce the amount of solar heat they reflect back into space.

译：气温上升将会使极地冰盖萎缩，北极冰盖正处于它历史记录的最低点，因而它们反射回空中的太阳热量会减少。

9号 ⭐️

Similarly, thawing of the permafrost lands of Alaska, Canada and Russia could release even more greenhouse gases, including methane, and further intensify global warming.

译：同样，阿拉斯加、加拿大和俄罗斯的永久冻土的融化可能会释放出包括甲烷在内的更多温室气体，并且进一步加剧全球变暖。

10号

By lowering the taxes of the donor and diminishing the tax revenues that would otherwise have been collected and partly distributed to rich and poor schools alike, federal and state governments are in effect subsidizing the charitable activity of parents who donate to their children’s school.

译：通过降低捐助者的纳税额，政府减少了本可以收缴上来并可将部分一视同仁地分配给富裕学校和贫困学校的税收收入，事实上，联邦政府和州政府是在资助这种父母为自己子女的学校进行捐款的慈善活动。

11号

The research of Till Von Watch, economist at Columbia University, suggests that not all people graduating into a recession see their life chances dimmed: those with degrees from elite universities catch up fairly quickly to where they otherwise would have been if they had graduated in better times; it is the masses beneath them that are left behind.

译：哥伦比亚大学的经济学家Till Von Watch所做的研究显示：并非所有毕业于衰退时期的大学生都会发现自己的人生机会会变得渺茫：那些拥有名牌大学学位的毕业生会很快达到他们毕业于经济状况良好时期时本应有的状态；而那些不如他们的普通院校毕业生则会被甩在后面。

12号

The crash was a major reason the U.S Fish and Wildlife Service （USFWS）decided to formally list the bird as threatened. “The lesser prairie chicken is in a desperate situation.” said USFWS Director Daniel Ashe.

译：数量骤减是美国鱼类及野生动物管理局决定正式将这一鸟类列入濒临动物的主要原因，“小草原榛鸡出境危机”，管理局局长丹尼尔·阿什说。

13号

Students with experience catch up after a few introductory courses，said Tom Cortina，the assistant dean at Carnegie Mellon’s School of Computer Science.

译：有经验的学生经过少量入门课程就能赶上，卡耐基梅隆大学计算机科学学院的副院长汤姆幕说。

14号 周日休息

15号

We have no more right to consume happiness without producing it than to consume wealth without producing it.

译：
①正如我们没有权利不创造财富就享用财富，同样，我们也没有权利不创造幸福却享用幸福。
②不创造幸福的人无权享用幸福，正如不创造财富的人无权享用财富一样。

16号

I shall define him as an individual who has elected as his primary duty and pleasure in life the activity of thinking in Socratic way about moral problems.

译：我会把知识分子定义为这样的人：他们会把用苏格拉底的方式来思考道德问题这样的行为视作是自己人生当中的首要职责和乐趣。

17号

Histories make men wise; poems witty; the mathematics subtle; natural philosophy deep; moral grave; logic and rhetoric able to contend.

译：读史使人明智，读诗使人聪慧，数学使人周密，自然哲学使人深刻，伦理学使人有修养，逻辑修辞使人善辩。

18号 ⭐️

In a world in which I don’t have to work, I might feel rather different — perhaps different enough to throw himself into a hobby or a passion project with the intensity usually reserved for professional matters.

译：在一个无需工作的世界，我的感受可能会相当不同——也许不同到他能用通常职业工作所特有的热情投身于某个爱好或者酷爱的项目中。

19号

But it doesn’t necessarily follow from findings like these that a world without work would be filled with unease.

译：但是这些发现并不一定意味着不用工作的世界将充满忧虑。

20号

Also, some research suggests that the explanation for rising rates of mortality, mental-health problems, and addiction among poorly-educated, middle-aged people is a shortage of well-paid jobs.

译：此外，一些研究显示，在受教育程度较低的中年人中，之所以过早死亡、患有心理疾病以及沉溺于毒品的比例不断升高就是因为缺乏高薪工作。

21号 周日休息

22号

Such visions are based on the downsides of being unemployed in a society built on the concept of employment.

译：这些设想所基于的前提是——在一个以就业观念为基础的社会里，人们失业所面临的种种弊端。

23号

At RoMan Manufacturing, a maker of electrical transformers and welding equipment that his father co-founded in 1980, Robert Roth keeps a close eye on the age of his nearly 200 workers. Five are retiring this year.

译：RoMan Manufacturing是一家电力变压器及焊接设备生产商，由Robert Roth的父亲于1980年与他人合伙创建，（如今）Robert Roth对其将近200名员工的年龄一直保持着密切关注。今年，有五位将要退休离职。

24号

If there is a role for government, it should really be getting involved in providing common goods — making sure there is space for playing fields and the money to pave tennis and netball courts, and encouraging the provision of all these activities in schools.

译：如果政府要发挥作用，它实际上应该参与提供公共设施——一方面确保有建设运动场地的空间和建设网球场和无挡板篮球场的资金，另一方面要鼓励学校开展所有这些体育运动。

25号 ⭐️

There is as much joy over a puffed-out first-timer being clapped over the line as there is about top talent shining.

译：气喘吁吁的初次参与者在跨越终点线时会得到人们的掌声喝彩，由此产生的快乐堪比顶尖天才光芒四射的表现。

26号

The Parkrun phenomenon began with a dozen friends and has inspired 400 events in the UK and more abroad.

译：园跑现象最初只有十二个朋友一起，如今在英国却已引发400个类似活动，在国外就更多了。

27号

Tronick himself is concerned that the worries about kids’ use of screens are born out of an “oppressive ideology that demands that parents should always be interacting” with their children.

译：Tronick（也可音译：特罗尼克）本人担心，对于孩子们使用带屏幕电子产品的各种担忧，是源自“一种压抑性的意识形态，这种意识形态要求父母应与自己的子女时刻保持互动交流”。

28号 周日休息

29号

More than three million doses were to be made available in early October 2009, though most of those initial doses were of the FluMist nasal spray type, which is not recommended for pregnant women, people over 50 or those with breathing difficulties, heart disease or several other problems.

译：到2009年10月初，300多万剂疫苗就能生产出来。然而，那些最初的疫苗大多是喷鼻型疫苗，不适合孕妇、50岁以上人群、呼吸障碍患者、心脏病患者以及患有其他病症的人群。

30号

Last month, Howard Schmidt, the nation’s cyber-czar, offered the federal government a proposal to make the web a safer place—a “voluntary trusted identity” system that would be the high-tech equivalent of a physical key, a fingerprint and a photo ID card, all rolled into one.

译：上个月，全国的互联网沙皇（网络大王）Howard Schmidt 给联邦政府提交了一份提案，建议加强网络安全。其内容是建立“自愿身份认证”系统，这种高科技技术等同于把有形的钥匙、指纹与带照片的身份证三者融合为一体。

31号

In effect, the approach would create a “walled garden” in cyberspace, with safe “neighborhoods” and bright “streetlights” to establish a sense of a trusted community.

译：事实上，这一方式将在互联网打造出一个“带围墙的花园”，它有着可靠的“邻居”以及明亮的“路灯”，形成了彼此有信任感的社区。
