Memorizing words

有道背单词词单：《Word List 6》

Favorite example

He enjoys a private life in the countryside.

译：他在乡下过着清静的生活。

The government is takeing steps to restrain inflation.

译：政府正在采取措施以抑制通货膨胀。

She constrained her mind not to wonder from the study.

译：她克制自己在学习时不分心。

You should’t try to suppress you feelings.

译：你不应该试图压抑自己感情。

Sometimes you’ve to check your emotions.

译：有时你必须抑制自己的感情。

Keep your curb on your temper. ⭐️

译：你得控制自己的脾气。

Governments makes laws and the police enforce them.

译：政府制定法律，警察执行法律。

She is ashamed to ask such a simple question.

译：她不好意思提这么简单的问题。

It’s the last despreate attempt to saving the company.

译：为挽救公司，这是最后所做的孤注一掷的尝试（努力）。

Your thoughtless behavior caused us considerable distress.

译：你不假思索（轻率）的行为给我们带来了极大的痛苦。
